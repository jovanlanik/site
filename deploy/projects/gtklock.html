Projects/gtklock

gtklock is a GTK-based lockscreen for Wayland and seems to be my most succesful project. It's available on many linux distributions.

I was dissatisfied with the options available at the time and decided to create an alternative by taking the UI of gtkgreet and writing a wlr-layer-shell/wlr-input-inhibitor lockscreen around it. It slowly grew with new features such as module support and was ported to the new ext-session-lock protocol.
